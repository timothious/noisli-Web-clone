What is Noisli?



Noisli is your digital place for focus.
With Noisli you can mix and match different sounds in order to create your perfect sound environment. We currently offer 28 high quality background sounds that you can play individually or combine with each other. You can adjust the volume for each sound in order to customize your combinations to your needs and make them your own.

Besides crafting your own Combos, you can also listen to the carefully curated Playlists so to be inspired and explore new sound suggestions for various situations. We offer a Timer that helps you work in sessions in order to prevent burnout, as well as a lovely distraction-free Text Editor.

Why Noisli works


Silence and loud noises are usually associated with danger, thus making us alert. It is the opposite of what we need when we want to focus or relax. Background noise on the other hand is a sound that doesnâ€™t contain spikes in volume, that is very constant and rather predictable keeping therefore our inner alarm systems calm.

Moreover, due to the high amount of different frequencies contained, background sounds have great masking abilities, reducing the negative impact of sudden spikes in external noise in our environment. While listening to such background sounds, every additional noise adds to the sounds youâ€™re already hearing and doesnâ€™t stand out so much anymore. Therefore, additional noises are blended in and you donâ€™t perceive them as disturbing as you normally would. This is also why music with lyrics can bring us easily out of focus, especially when having to deal with tasks that involve writing or reading. Lastly, the right amount of background noise, not too quiet and not too loud, can also help you to think in a more abstract way enhancing creative thinking.
